My Coding Journey: From C++ Enthusiast to Full Stack Developer

GitHub repo with my C++ code

In the ever-evolving realm of programming, my journey began on February 23, 2021, when I took my first steps into the world of C++. Armed with curiosity and determination, I embarked on a self-taught adventure that has shaped my trajectory as a coder. Year 0 - 2: Mastering the Fundamentals in C++ During the initial phase of my coding odyssey, I delved deep into the intricacies of C++. I established my coding foundation through various resources, including the comprehensive lessons from www.learncpp.com, the insightful guidance of "C++ Primer Plus 6th Edition" by Stephen Prata (2011), and the wealth of knowledge provided by YouTube tutorials. In addition, I explored cppreference.com and actively engaged with GitHub repositories

To demonstrate my progress and share my code snippets, I proudly created my first GitHub repository dedicated to my C++ projects. This repository stands not only as a testament to my learning journey but also as a contribution to the collective knowledge of my future coding reference.

Eager to broaden my skill set, I expanded my coding repertoire to include HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With newfound proficiency, I crafted my inaugural static website—a personal portfolio showcasing my evolving skills and projects. This marked a pivotal moment as I transitioned from a C++ enthusiast to a budding Full Stack Developer. Simultaneously, I immersed myself in the world of databases, SQL Server Management Studio and SQL.

Present Role: Section Manager with IT Project Focus

In my current capacity as a Section Manager, I lead with a unique blend of managerial acumen and technical expertise. This role not only sharpens my organizational skills but also allows me to apply my IT prowess to real-world challenges. My journey from C++ initiation to Full Stack Developer reflects not only my technical growth but also my commitment to embracing new challenges and opportunities in the ever-expanding world of programming.
